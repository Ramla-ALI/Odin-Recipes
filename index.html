Chocolate Cake Recipe

Description

This rich and moist chocolate cake is a perfect dessert for chocolate lovers, topped with a decadent chocolate ganache.

Ingredients

  • 1 and 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 and 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 and 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 and 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans.
  2. In a large bowl, mix the dry ingredients: flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, and baking soda.
  3. Add the eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla to the dry mixture. Beat until smooth.
  4. Stir in the boiling water (batter will be thin).
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared pans and bake for 30-35 minutes.
  6. Cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then transfer to wire racks to cool completely.
  7. Top with your favorite frosting or chocolate ganache before serving.
