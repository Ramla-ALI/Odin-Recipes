Lasagna Recipe

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is a comforting dish made with layers of rich, meaty sauce, creamy béchamel, and tender pasta. Perfect for family dinners or gatherings, it's sure to impress with its hearty and flavorful taste.

Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish thats been enjoyed for centuries, combining the best of pasta, cheese, and sauce. With this recipe, you’ll learn how to create the perfect lasagna at home.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 4 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil and sauté the onions and garlic until soft. Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in the crushed tomatoes and tomato paste, season with salt and pepper, and let the sauce simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. In a large pot, cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  5. In a baking dish, spread a layer of sauce, followed by a layer of noodles, then a layer of ricotta and mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers until you’ve used all ingredients.
  6. Top the final layer with Parmesan cheese and bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until bubbly and golden brown on top.
  7. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
