Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe

Description

This classic Spaghetti Carbonara is made with simple ingredients like eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper to create a creamy, savory pasta dish.

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 200g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 100g Pecorino Romano cheese, grated
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of salted boiling water.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, fry the pancetta in a pan until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, beat the eggs and mix in the grated Pecorino cheese.
  4. Drain the spaghetti, reserving a bit of the pasta water. Toss the spaghetti in the pan with pancetta.
  5. Remove the pan from heat, then add the egg and cheese mixture, stirring quickly to prevent scrambling.
  6. Add some reserved pasta water if the sauce is too thick. Season with freshly ground black pepper and serve immediately.
